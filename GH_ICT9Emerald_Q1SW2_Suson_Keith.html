Noli Me Tangere Characters

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Y Magsalin Maria Clara Padre Damaso Tenyente Guvarra Kapitan Tiago
He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra. He studied in Spain for 7 years. The daughter of Kapitan Tiago. Dressed in Filipina, bearer of a beautiful smile and eyes that portray goodwill. She is the woman bethroned to marry Crisostomo Ibarra. Padre Damaso is a Franciscan priest. He is known to be an arrogant person to talk to. A close friend of Don Rafael Ibarra. He was by Don Rafael's side during his times of hardship. Kapitan Tiago does not identify himself as an Indio. He is known to be the richest in all of Binundok, now known as Binundo.
